ABOUT US




Phoe Wa Travel & Tour Co.,Ltd is one of the leading travel agents in Myanmar, registered under the Ministry of Hotels and Tourism with license no. Kha 1006. We are a 100% Myanmar owned private company. Since our establishment in 2004, we have filled our valued client’s wishes for a perfect holiday to destinations throughout our peaceful and beautiful country. We offer a full range of travel service from your first arrival to the time of your departure. We manage individual or group tours, whether they be specialized or just people wanting a great vacation.



We are proud to boast of having an excellent track record and we are an active member of the UMTA (Union of Myanmar Travel Association) and JATA (Japan Association of Travel Agents). Phoe Wa Travel & Tour Co.,Ltd has a strong financial background and enjoys special contract rates with our suppliers: hotels, airlines, cruise operators, restaurants, etc. With a branch office at each of the major destinations such as Bagan, Mandalay, Taunggyi and Sittwe, we are able to provide our clients with a truly personalized service and our staff can be reached 24 hours a day at each destinatio



The company is committed to achieve “Total Customer Satisfaction”. Our team of enthusiastic, professional staff and multi-language tour guides are always ready to assist you efficiently and effectively. Our service is famous for our prompt customer response and our sincere personal service. It is because of our esteemed staff, multi-language tour guides and our after-hours emergency contact availability that we have established an excellent reputation both within Myanmar and our client’s home countries.



We are totally committed to our motto “Your Premium Travel Specialist”.
